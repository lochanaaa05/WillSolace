Will Solace

Son of Apollo

Will Solace is a skilled healer and son of Apollo. As the head of the Apollo cabin at Camp Half-Blood, he is known for his calm demeanor, healing abilities, and leadership qualities. Will has bright blonde hair, blue eyes, and a sunny personality that reflects his divine parentage.

Abilities

  • Healing
  • Archery
  • Light Manipulation
  • Music
